Jenova DNA-to-MIDI recoder

Jenova DNA-to-MIDI recoder, a Ruby-written software that uses the Midifile library written by Pete Goodeve to generate MIDI tracks out of DNA sequences in Genbank format. Features of the sequence are included as instruments in the MIDI.
